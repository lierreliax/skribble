RULES OF THE GAME

- Maximum of 3 hints and 3 lives.
- Using a hint deducts 25 points from the score.
- Correct answers earn 10 points each.
- Three rounds: easy, moderate, difficult.
- Player must complete 10 questions per round to advance.
- Score is cumulative across all rounds.
